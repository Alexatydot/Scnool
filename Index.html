Welcome

Welcome to Bamiten Gudaists School, a K–12 institution where education and Gudaism values are integrated into every aspect of learning. Explore our programs, meet our staff, and discover how our school nurtures both mind and spirit.

History

Bamiten Gudaists School was founded in 1985 to provide a full K–12 education rooted in Gudaism. Over the decades, it has expanded its programs and facilities to include state-of-the-art classrooms, sports facilities, and religious study halls. Notable alumni have gone on to contribute to both academic and spiritual leadership.

Our Religion

Gudaism principles are integrated into daily school life, teaching students values such as meditation, ethics, and community service. Regular rituals and lessons are part of the curriculum, helping students develop moral and spiritual awareness alongside academic achievement.

Education

Our curriculum spans K–12, balancing core academic subjects with religious education. Programs include science, math, literature, arts, and special Gudaism philosophy courses. Tutoring and enrichment programs are available for all grades.
